**Tagline:** Instantly detect scam messages and phishing links before they cost you money.

Millions of people fall victim to scams every day via WhatsApp messages, SMS, emails, and fake websites. Most users cannot quickly verify if a message is legitimate, leading to financial loss and compromised personal data.

## üí° Solution
ProofCheck AI is a **fast, AI-powered fraud detection system** that combines **rule-based pattern detection** with **intelligent AI analysis**. Users can paste a message or URL and receive:

- **Instant risk classification:** Low / Medium / High  
- **Explainable results:** Highlights risky phrases and patterns  
- **Actionable safety advice:** Ignore, block, or report  

The hybrid system ensures **speed, accuracy, and reliability**, even if AI services are temporarily unavailable.
